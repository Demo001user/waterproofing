below grade

At Al Mamaar Waterproofing and Insulation, our team of experts specializes in providing critical waterproofing solutions in various areas. When it comes to below-grade waterproofing, we offer a range of effective options to protect structures from water infiltration. These options include liquid membrane applications, where a seamless coating is applied to create a waterproof barrier. Sheet membranes are another option, offering durable and flexible protection against moisture. Additionally, we provide reinforced flexible membranes that offer enhanced strength and longevity. Our injection systems and chemical grouting services help seal and repair cracks, ensuring a watertight seal. Crystalline waterproofing is also available for negative side waterproofing, providing long-lasting protection. Lastly, we offer drainage systems to effectively manage water flow and prevent water accumulation. With our expertise and diverse range of waterproofing solutions, we can address the specific needs of any project.
